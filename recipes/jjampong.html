Jjampong

Description

A delicious spicy korean-chinese seafood noodle soup with shrimp, scallions, and crab.

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce Anchovies
  • 1 piece of 6x6 dried kelp
  • 12 cups of water
  • large green onion
  • 2 ounces leek, cut into 1/2 2 inch strips
  • 2 ounces bok choy
  • 3 large cabbage leaves
  • 4 ounces onion
  • one small carrot
  • 8 mussels
  • 4 large shrimp
  • 4 ounces squid
  • 24 clams
  • 4 ounces of thin beef or pork
  • 1 pound of jajangmyeon noodles
  • gochujang
  • sesame oil
  • minced garlic
  • minced ginger
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. water, anchovies, and kelp in large pot for 40 minutes
  2. strain the stock
  3. combine hot pepper flakes and 1 tsp toasted sesame oil into small bowl
  4. mix with spoon
  5. put wok over high heat, adding vegetable oil, garlic, ginger and stir 20 seconds
  6. add beef to wok
  7. mix all ingredients excluding stock
  8. add vegetables
  9. add stock and seafood
  10. cook noodles
  11. ENJOY!
