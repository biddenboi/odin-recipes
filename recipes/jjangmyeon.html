Jjampong

Description

Black bean noodle soup comprised of vegetables and beef/pork.

Ingredients

  • jjangmyeon noodles
  • 1/2 pound pork belly
  • 1 pound of korean radish
  • 1 cup of zucchini
  • 1 cup of potato
  • 1 1/2 cups of onion chunks
  • 3 tbsp of vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp and 1/4 cup 0f korean black bean paste
  • 2 tbsp of potato starch powder with 1/4 cup water and 1 tbsp of sugar mixed in a small bowl
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup cucumber

Steps

  1. stir fry pork belly, tbsp vegetable oil, in deep wok pan until golden brown and crispy
  2. pour out pork fat
  3. add radish, stir 1 minute
  4. add potato, onion, zuccini, and stir for 3 minutes
  5. 2 TBSP of vegetable oil added, then 1/4 cup of black bean paste
  6. stir repeatedly
  7. 2 cups of water and let simmer and cook with lid closed
  8. open lid and taste a sample, adjust if not thickened
  9. add sesame oil
  10. ENJOY!
